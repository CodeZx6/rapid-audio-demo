PACE: listening examples

Training free physical prior conditioning for frozen diffusion based speech enhancement.

Fifteen utterances, three per benchmark. Each row plays the noisy input, the frozen backbone at its default number of function evaluations, the same backbone with +PACE at a reduced number of evaluations, and the clean reference. All clips are 16 kHz mono. Examples were selected where +PACE improves both the intrusive metric (PESQ against the clean reference) and non intrusive perceptual scores (UTMOS, DNSMOS) over the backbone.
